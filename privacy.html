Privacy Policy for The Still Place

Effective date: March 20, 2026

The Still Place respects your privacy.

Information collected

The Still Place is designed to keep your use simple and personal. At this time, the app does not require account creation or login.

The app may store information locally on your device, including:

  • Selected Bible version
  • Reminder preferences
  • Selected path
  • Journal reflections, prayers, and action steps saved in app history

How your information is used

Your information is used only to support the app’s features, including:

  • Saving your preferences
  • Saving your entries in app history
  • Scheduling local reminder notifications

Data sharing

The Still Place does not sell your data.

The Still Place does not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, The Still Place may send local reminders based on the reminder settings you choose in the app.

Data storage

Your app data is stored locally on your device unless Apple or your device settings back it up as part of your normal device backup process.

Your choices

  • Change reminder settings in the app
  • Turn notifications off in iPhone Settings
  • Delete saved app data by removing the app from your device

Children’s privacy

The Still Place is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
